About Us

China House is an authentic Chinese restaurant located in the heart of Rome, New York. We offer a vast menu rich with traditional Chinese cuisine, ranging from daily special combinations such as General Tso's Chicken, Kung Pao Chicken, and Shrimp Egg Foo Young, to a variety of appetizers, soups, and dishes featuring fried rice, lo mein, chow mein, and more. Our signature dishes include the Pu Pu Platter, Szechuan Shrimp, and our unique House Special Triple Delight. We pride ourselves on providing a memorable dining experience with fresh, flavorful food that pays tribute to the culinary traditions of China. Whether you're looking for a family dinner, a quick takeout, or a catered event, China House in Rome, NY is here to serve you!
